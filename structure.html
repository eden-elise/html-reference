  1. COMP 310 — Operating Systems
  2. Web Programming — this assignment
  3. Linear Algebra II — Axler based
  4. Geometry

Linear algebra and quantum computing share a lot of notation. Once you understand vector spaces properly, bra-ket notation starts making much more sense.

Key Terms

UTM
A virtual machine app for macOS that runs Ubuntu locally. Used for all COMP 310 work.
SSH
Secure Shell — used to connect to USD lab machines remotely with RSA key-based authentication.
mmap()
A system call used in COMP 310 to map files directly into memory. Used heavily in the EXT2 filesystem project.
Bra-ket notation
Dirac notation used in quantum mechanics and quantum computing to represent quantum states. |ψ⟩ is a ket, ⟨ψ| is a bra.

FAQ

How do you manage five courses at once?

Poorly! ADHD is a bitch but with good tooling. I try to time blocking helps more than anything else, because dedicated blocks for each subject rather than switching between them constantly is better for my brain

Do you use an LLM for homework?

For this HTML assignment yes, as a guide. For operating systems I also use it as a like "second professor" because the teacher struggles to make stuff understandable. For linear algebra and geometry llms do not do that well.

What is the hardest course this semester?

COMP 310 at 7pm on Monday and Wednesday nights after a full day of classes SUCKS. The content is interesting but the timing is brutal. Linear Algebra II is the hardest conceptually, and I am not doing too well, but I think that's more because I am not doing well juggling life not that I am not capable of learning it.
